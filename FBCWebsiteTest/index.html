Our Approach

We are a freestanding birth center offering complete maternity care by attentive, licensed professionals. From early pregnancy through the postpartum period, we assist by providing comprehensive maternity care, education, counseling, support and more. Let us help you create the birth experience you want.

Providing you with a comfortable, home-like environment.

Your prenatal appointments will take place in our soothing office environment with complimentary tea and water. Telehealth appointments are available as needed. Lab draws are done within the comfort of the birth center during your scheduled appointments. We will order any needed ultrasounds to be done a location convenient to you.

Meet the Team

Dawn Meier, Licensed Midwife

Dawn earned a psychology degree from Wright University. After studying birth trauma, she was inspired to facilitate peaceful, natural birth and she became a doula. Shortly after, Dawn decided to become a direct-entry midwife and she enrolled at Manna School of Midwifery and Health Sciences.

Dawn attended many births in homes, clinics, and hospitals throughout Southwest Florida, Jamaica and Guatemala as a part of her training. She opened a small homebirth practice and also began working as a staff midwife at the Family Birth Centers (Naples & Fort Myers)

March of 2005, she took over The Family Birth Center of Naples and is now the owner and Director. She has studied homeopathy at Florida Academy of Classical Homeopathy, hoping to eventually add this amazing modality of holistic healthcare into her existing practice.
